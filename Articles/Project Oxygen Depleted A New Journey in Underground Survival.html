Project Oxygen Depleted: A New Journey in Underground Survival

30/12/2024

Project Oxygen Depleted is an upcoming strategy-based survival game currently in development. Set in a post-apocalyptic world where the surface has become uninhabitable, humanity is forced to live underground. As the leader of a group of survivors, your task is to guide your people through the perilous depths of the earth, rebuilding civilization and ensuring survival in an environment that is as dangerous as it is fragile.
 

In this game, players will be responsible for constructing a thriving subterranean society. You'll dig caverns, build critical infrastructure, and manage essential resources like food, water, and, most importantly, oxygen. The underground is full of challenges: resource scarcity, environmental hazards, and the constant threat of unknown dangers. Every decision you make will impact your people's survival and the future of humanity.
 

Your leadership will be tested as you balance technological advancement with the immediate needs of your citizens. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities and technologies, but your progress won't come without difficulty. Defending your underground civilization from environmental dangers and other threats is just as important as managing your resources.
