Project Compendium

Latest Project

15/07/2025

Discover a World of Strategy and Mystery in This Pixel-Art Card Game

In the ever-expanding landscape of strategy card games, a unique title stands out by blending classic deck-building with an innovative tile-based system. This pixel-art strategy card game invites players into a cryptic world, where every tile placed teaches elemental powers, reveals strange creatures, and unlocks the secrets of a forgotten civilization.
 

Unlike traditional card games, this experience integrates the game board as a dynamic battlefield and a map to ancient lore. Each tile is more than a playing piece — it’s a key to hidden knowledge and new strategic possibilities. Players begin with a modest deck, but as they progress, they unlock elemental nodes that shape their deck-building and tactical decisions.
 

The creatures encountered aren’t mere cards; they are echoes from a lost world, each adding depth to an ever-growing compendium. Collecting these beings is essential, as the compendium serves both as a record of progress and a gateway to the full narrative.
